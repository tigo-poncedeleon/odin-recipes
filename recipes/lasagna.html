Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

Make my grandma's time-tested lasagna recipe right from the comfort of your own house!
It should take around an hour to fully prepare

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt, pepper, Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles until tender, then drain.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a pan, season, and stir in marinara sauce.
  3. Mix ricotta cheese with the egg in a bowl.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 45 minutes, then let it rest before serving.
