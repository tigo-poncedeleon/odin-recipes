Vodka Pasta Recipe

Home

Description

Whip up this creamy, flavorful vodka pasta without leaving your kitchen!
It comes together in about 30 minutes for a perfect weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz pasta (penne or rigatoni)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce (or crushed tomatoes)
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • ¼ cup vodka
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (to taste)
  • Fresh basil (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water until al dente, then drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Pour in vodka and let it simmer briefly, then stir in tomato sauce.
  4. Add heavy cream and Parmesan cheese, seasoning with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  5. Toss in the pasta until coated, garnish with basil, and serve warm.
